Q1: What is one tool I can invest in to remove carbon dioxide from industries?



Robin: Carbon capture can capture 90 percent of carbon dioxide from factories.

Q2: What are some alternatives I can invest in to reduce emissions from electricity.



Robin: Solar and wind are key to reducing emissions in the electricity sector which is the single largest source of carbon dioxide emissions.

Q3: How can I make industries shift to more greener technology



Robin: Money incentives to switch to green technology, laws to reduce emissions, and taxes on industries that emit a lot of carbon will force industries to adapt and make reducing emissions a priority.
