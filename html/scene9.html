Evelyn: Thank you so much for meeting me, and all the efforts you put into raising awareness on climate change.

Aeryn: You’re welcome, if we don’t fix this issue now it will just get so much worse.

Evelyn: As you might know, the previous prime minister was very much against taking any measures to solve this issue.

Evelyn: While it might seem like he was uneducated about climate change, he was actually knowledgeable on the subject, but chose to turn a blind eye to it in hopes the people would too.

Evelyn: Turning a blind is easier especially with all the ties we have in the government to huge corporations and industries creating these issues.

Robin: What are your plans to change this?

Evelyn: This is where I wanted to get your input, mind if I ask you some question?

Aeryn: Of course!

Q1: What is one tool I can invest in to remove carbon dioxide from industries?


Robin: Carbon capture can capture 90 percent of carbon dioxide from factories.

Q2: What are some alternatives I can invest in to reduce emissions from electricity.


Robin: Solar and wind are key to reducing emissions in the electricity sector which is the single largest source of carbon dioxide emissions.

Q3: How can I make industries shift to more greener technology


Robin: Money incentives to switch to green technology, laws to reduce emissions, and taxes on industries that emit a lot of carbon will force industries to adapt and make reducing emissions a priority.

Evelyn: Our talk today was very informative and I can see you both have great passion for the environment. I believe when making decisions for this country it is important to work together with the people and scientists.

Evelyn: With that being said I wanted to give you both the opportunity to work alongside me in raising awareness and researching this climate problem at the federal level. Together I want to create a brighter and greener future together in Canada. How does that sound?

Scientist: That sounds incredible! I would love to join.

Aeryn: Same here! I feel like this would be fulfilling my purpose on Earth.

Evelyn: There is a short contract you would need to sign, but take a read I promise there are no ulterior motives, I am 100% committed to working with you guys in solving the climate problem.
