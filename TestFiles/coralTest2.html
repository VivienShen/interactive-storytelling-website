Climate change is a great threat to sea turtles. The sea level rising and the stronger storms will destroy their beach habitats. Warming oceans also change ocean currents which introduces the sea turtles to new predators and harms the coral reef that they need to survive. Climate Change Puts Pressure on Sea Turtles

Many fishes depend on corals for their food and habitat, while corals depend on fishes to feed on them for them to continue reproducing and remove some diseases. Increased ocean temperatures from climate change will affect the behavior of coral reef fishes.
